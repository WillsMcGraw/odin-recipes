Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

This is the best chocolate chip cookies recipe ever! No funny ingredients, no chilling time, etc. Just a simple, straightforward, amazingly delicious, doughy yet still fully cooked, chocolate chip cookie that turns out perfectly every single time!

This is a recipe that will give you some delicious oatmeal raisin cookies for you to enjoy on a nice day off! Do enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Flour
  • Sea Salt
  • Baking Powder & Soda
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Mix the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Mix the butter and sugards until combined.
  4. Beat in the eggs and vanilla until light.
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Roll the mixture into balls roughly 1 inch in size.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes. Rmeove when they barely start to turn brown.
  9. Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cool
